Our story
"Iléri" is Yoruba for promise. Our promise is simple: African healthcare professionals deserve apparel made with the same care they give their patients.

We don't compete on price. We compete on fit, comfort, design, quality and attention to detail — beginning in Nigeria, then Ghana, Kenya, South Africa and the diaspora. This isn't a uniform supplier. It's a performance apparel company for the people who hold healthcare together.
